Rslidy

  • Drop-in replacement for W3C Slidy and Slidy2.
  • Responsive HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (TypeScript).
  • No other dependencies.
  • Lightweight.

Overview of Rslidy's Features

  • Slide Viewer:
    • Animated slide transitions.
    • Overview panel.
    • Table of contents panel.
    • Navigable progress bar.
    • Image viewer with pan + zoom.
    • All slides view.
    • Low light mode.
    • Help page.
  • Responsive Layout:
    • Portrait + landscape.
    • Print style sheet.
  • Responsive Interaction:
    • Swipe, pinch, tap.
    • Tilt, shake.
    • Keyboard.
    • Mouse pointer, mouse wheel.

Slide Viewer

Settings

  • Toggle features.
  • Adjust font sizes.
  • Low light mode.

Image Viewer

Accessibility

  • ARIA roles and properties, updated at runtime.
  • Accessible HTML5 elements, such as <nav> and <main>.
  • Fully navigable with keyboard shortcuts.
  • Font sizes are freely adjustable.
  • Screen reader friendly. (Tested with JAWS and NVDA. Special thanks go to Christopher Kopel.)

Gesture Support

  • Swipe.
  • Margin tap (similar to that of e-readers).
  • Tilt/Tip.
  • Shake.
